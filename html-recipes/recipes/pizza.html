Pizza

An old classic!

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, etc., which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Olives
  • Bell peppers

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°C (480°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, olives, and bell peppers.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy!
