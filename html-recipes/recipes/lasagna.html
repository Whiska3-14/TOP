Lasagna

What a delicatessen!

Lasagna is a type of pasta that comes in large, flat sheets. It's typically layered with delicious ingredients like meat in bolognese sauce and creamy béchamel sauce. It's a hearty and comforting dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Meat
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Béchamel sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Boil the lasagna pasta in a pot with water and salt for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare the meat and the sauces.
  4. Layer the lasagna pasta, meat, bolognese sauce, and béchamel sauce in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat the layers until the baking dish is full.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy!
